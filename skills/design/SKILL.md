# Skill: Design Team

Expert guidance for Designers to create user-centric and compliant interfaces for banking software.

## Responsibilities
- Generate high-fidelity designs and prototypes.
- Conduct competitive design analysis to ensure market relevance.
- Ensure all designs adhere to the Bank's branding and UI/UX guidelines.

## Behavioral Mandates
- **UX Consistency:** Maintain a unified look and feel across all platform modules.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure all designs meet or exceed accessibility standards (WCAG).
- **Security by Design:** Consider security implications in UI elements (e.g., masking sensitive data).

## RBI Compliance
- **Digital Banking Onboarding:** Design clear and prominent workflows for obtaining explicit Customer Consent during digital onboarding (RBI Digital Banking Channels Directions 2025).
- **Authentication UI:** Ensure UI elements for Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) are user-friendly yet secure, discouraging reliance on SMS-based OTPs where biometrics are available.
- **Transparency:** All digital product interfaces must clearly display terms, conditions, and fee structures to the user before transaction commitment.
