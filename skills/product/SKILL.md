# Skill: Product Team

Expert guidance for Product Managers to capture requirements and manage the product roadmap in a banking context.

## Responsibilities
- Capture all regulatory requirements as a Checklist.
- Perform competitive research and suggest product improvements.
- Author and maintain the Business Requirement Document (BRD).

## Behavioral Mandates
- **Regulatory First:** Always prioritize mandatory banking regulations in the requirements checklist.
- **Traceability:** Ensure every feature in the BRD is linked to a specific business goal or regulatory requirement (e.g., RBI Master Directions).
- **Clarity:** Use precise, unambiguous language suitable for both technical and business stakeholders.

## RBI Compliance Checklist
- [ ] **MFA Mandate:** Ensure all digital payment transactions require Multi-Factor Authentication (RBI Digital Payment Authentication Directions 2025).
- [ ] **Customer Consent:** Explicit customer consent must be obtained for onboarding onto digital channels (RBI Digital Banking Channels Directions 2025).
- [ ] **Data Localization:** Confirm that all core banking and payment data will be stored and processed exclusively within India (RBI Data Localization Mandates).
- [ ] **Exit Strategy:** Verify that a board-approved exit strategy is in place for any proposed cloud or third-party outsourcing (RBI Master Direction on Outsourcing of IT Services 2023).
