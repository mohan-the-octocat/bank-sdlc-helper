# RBI Regulatory Requirement Database

This document serves as a structured database of Reserve Bank of India (RBI) guidelines and mandates relevant to application development within the financial sector.

## Cyber Security
### IT Governance and Cyber Resilience
- **Board Accountability:** Mandates a Board-level IT Strategy Committee (ITSC) and a Senior Management-level IT Steering Committee. The Board is directly responsible for IT strategy, risk management, and business continuity.
- **CISO Role:** Requires the appointment of a sufficiently senior Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) with a direct reporting line to the Board/ITSC.
- **Cyber Resilience:** Focuses on "Zero Trust" principles, continuous monitoring, and a Cyber Crisis Management Plan (CCMP).
- **Secure SDLC:** Mandates secure application development lifecycles and real-time threat defense.
- **Source:** RBI Master Direction on IT Governance, Risk, Controls and Assurance Practices (2023), Effective April 1, 2024.

## Digital Banking
### Digital Payment Authentication and Channel Onboarding
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Mandates 2FA for all digital transactions, encouraging methods beyond SMS-based OTPs like biometrics or hardware tokens.
- **Customer Consent:** Requires explicit customer consent for onboarding onto digital channels.
- **Network Independence:** Ensures digital services are accessible across all mobile operators.
- **Source:** RBI Digital Payment Authentication Directions (2025) & Digital Banking Channels Directions (2025).

## Outsourcing and Cloud
### Managed Services and Data Localization
- **Material Outsourcing:** REs must identify "material" services that significantly impact business operations or customer data.
- **Audit Rights:** REs and the RBI must have unhindered access to the service provider’s data, systems, and records for audits.
- **Exit Strategy:** Mandates a board-approved policy for smooth transition of services in case of vendor failure.
- **Data Localization:** All core banking and payment data must be stored and processed exclusively within India.
- **Shared Responsibility:** RE remains fully accountable for data protection and regulatory compliance even when using cloud providers.
- **Source:** RBI Master Direction on Outsourcing of IT Services (2023) & RBI Data Localization Mandates.
