# RBI Compliance Checklist: Product Definition

Use this checklist during the Product Definition and BRD drafting phase to ensure regulatory compliance.

## Requirement Gathering
- [ ] **Explicit Customer Consent:** Have we defined the mechanism for obtaining explicit consent for digital onboarding?
- [ ] **MFA by Design:** Does the product requirement include Multi-Factor Authentication for all digital transactions?
- [ ] **Data Scope:** Have we identified all "Sensitive Personal Data or Information" (SPDI) that the application will handle?

## Vendor and Service Strategy
- [ ] **Materiality Assessment:** If using third-party services, has a materiality assessment been conducted?
- [ ] **Audit Readiness:** Do our requirements mandate unhindered audit access for RBI/Bank to any third-party systems?
